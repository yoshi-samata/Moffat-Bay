OUR STORY


Moffat Bay Marina opened in 1945. What started as a dream for Linus and Rebecca Snoot turned into a reality
when John H Riley, the wealthy industrialist and current owner of the marina decided to sell his possessions after
his spiritual adviser led him to believe the worls was ending. Luckily for Linus and Rebecca this meant they could purchace
the Marina at an end of the world price. Riley tried to prevent the tranfer of ownership after realizing the world didn't end
but his attempts to retain ownership through litigation were unsuccesful. Allowing the Snoots to own the Marina they had
had leased a slip in for years. The Snoots began updateing the Marina right away
The Snoots worked tirlessly to improve the facilty as did their children, Bob and Sally
Snoot who would add the lodge in 1967.Over the years development continued until it became the luxurious
Marina you see today. So while you are enjoying your stay, raise a glass to the Snoots who made it all possible.
